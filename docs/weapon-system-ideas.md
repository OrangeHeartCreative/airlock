# Weapon System Ideas — Prisoner: Starship Bloom

## Current Implementation Starter Set (Build Next)

- **Shiv Pistol (Kinetic Sidearm)**
  - Role: Reliable baseline weapon.
  - Strengths: Consistent damage, easy ammo economy, good general use.
  - Trade-offs: Average crowd control, medium noise.

- **Incinerator Carbine**
  - Role: Close-range crowd and hazard-clearing weapon.
  - Strengths: Excellent against vine growth, trap-clearing, damage-over-time potential.
  - Trade-offs: High resource drain, raises enemy aggro quickly, short effective range.

- **UV Arc Cutter**
  - Role: Anti-mutation / anti-regeneration specialist.
  - Strengths: Sustained beam pressure, strong vs boss phases and healing enemies.
  - Trade-offs: Heat/overheat management required, weaker burst if mismanaged.

## Additional Weapon Concepts (Keep for Future Scope)

- **Spore Needlegun**
  - Quiet precision weapon with high weak-point payoff.

- **Pulse Shotgun**
  - High close-range stagger and knockback, loud and slower cadence.

- **Bio-Acid Sprayer**
  - Area denial via corrosive pools, strong in chokepoints.

- **Rail Lance (Charged)**
  - High-risk line-penetration shot with charge-time vulnerability.

- **Grav Mine Launcher**
  - Tactical utility launcher to slow/group enemies for combos.

- **Pollen Disruptor**
  - Support tool that debuffs enemy accuracy/speed in an area.

- **Emergency Flare Gun**
  - Panic/utility weapon for threat disruption and brief visibility control.

## Design Direction Notes

- Balance weapons around **risk, noise, and bio-reactivity**.
- Preserve survival-horror tension by ensuring no weapon removes resource pressure.
- Favor distinct identity per weapon over minor stat variants.
- Keep future weapons modular so they can be added incrementally after core combat feels solid.
