# Airlock: The Oncoming Swarm

**Survive the swarm. Upgrade your arsenal. Escape the station.**

*Airlock: The Oncoming Swarm* is a relentless, top-down sci-fi survival shooter that tasks you with fighting your way through 12 increasingly brutal sectors of a fully compromised space station. The infestation is spreading quickly, and your life support is failing.

You must navigate choking corridors, eliminate sprawling alien hive nodes, and manage your rapidly depleting oxygen and contamination levels. Retreat to the safe room to breathe, re-arm with randomized weapon drops—ranging from the high-heat UV Arc Cutter to the devastating Pulse Shotgun—and extract before the swarm overwhelms you. 

Featuring a completely procedural, synthesized heavy-metal and atmospheric drone soundtrack, *Airlock* delivers maximum tension in a lightweight web package.

## Features

* **12 Gauntlet Sectors:** Evolving layouts, escalating enemy pressure, and visual themes to survive.
* **Intense Survival Mechanics:** Juggling health is only half the battle. Watch your oxygen meters; once you run out of air, contamination sets in and eats away at your life.
* **5 Distinct Weapon Types:** Scavenge weapon crates for the Shiv Pistol, Incinerator Carbine, and more, balancing ammo, fuel, and heat.
* **Procedural Heavy-Metal Audio:** A fully synthesized background music engine that reacts to the game state—ambient drones in the menus, and driving drums with power chords in the thick of the swarm.
* **One-Stick Combat:** Visceral, streamlined movement and facing controls keep the focus purely on positioning and situational awareness. 

## Controls (Gamepad Required)

*Airlock is designed exclusively for gamepads. Keyboard input is intentionally disabled.*

* **Move & Aim:** Left Stick
* **Fire Weapon:** Right Trigger (RT) / Right Bumper (RB)
* **Interact / Select:** A Button
* **Back:** B Button 
* **Pause:** Start / Menu Button
* *(Note: Controls can be completely remapped inside the in-game Settings menu).*
